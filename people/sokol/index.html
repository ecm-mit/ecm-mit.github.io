PhD, Chemistry, University of Cambridge, UK, 2019
Thesis: Photoelectrochemical tandem cells with enzymes wired to hierarchically-structured electrodes for solar fuel synthesis
MChem with a Year Abroad, Chemistry, University of Edinburgh, UK, 2014
Year Abroad: Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, 2012-2013

Katarzyna's current research focuses on the development of new generation all solid-state Li-based batteries and memristors using solution-phase processing and fabrication techniques. Her research expertise lies in (photo)electrochemical device design and assembly, artificial photosynthesis and solar fuels, nanotechnology, nanomaterial synthesis and colloidal science, as well as functional and energy material synthesis and characterisation. Her other interests revolve around biomimetic materials, flexible and wearable electronics, multifunctional and stimuli-responsive coatings.
