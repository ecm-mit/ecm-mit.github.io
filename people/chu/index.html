Thesis: The role of electrolyte anions on lithium sulfide growth realizing high-performance lithium-sulfur batteries
BSc, Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering, Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology, Republic of Korea, 2017

Hyunwon is working on the development of solid-state batteries, their material, structure, and device design. In his work, he is developing the model systems to understand the grain boundary transport phenomena, and ultimately aiming to control the ionic and electronic transport properties and engineer the interface for better energy storage.
